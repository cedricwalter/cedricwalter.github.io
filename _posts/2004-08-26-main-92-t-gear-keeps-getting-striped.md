 | **Symptoms**: - Main 92T gear keeps getting stripped. - Teeth broke in the main gear by just doing a fast descent                                                                                                                                                     |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Reasons**- The one way bearing will sometimes slip, and then catch again. This allows the engine to rev, and when the the bearing grips again, the shock load breaks the teeth on the main gear. The one way bearing is the weak link in the Caliber design. . Keep |
| **Cure**: - replace regurly the main 92T gear - Clean the one way bearing and or replace it                                                                                                                                                                           |
