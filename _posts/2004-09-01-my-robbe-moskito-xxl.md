NOTE : The whole helicopter and especially the blade will be repaint with a purple color, because i can’t see the disk while flying…

# equipped with**

- Futaba 149DP dual
- JR 8700G : tail super servo
- 4000 : collective servo
- 3 x 4231 : others servo
- Gyroscope CSM 540
- 2 battery + dual switch
- 2000 ma + 2000 ma Rossi 53
- Novarossi forward facing pipe NHP 62 cm Main blades
- NHP tail blade Gear upgrade
- Contest clutch
- Driven Tail
