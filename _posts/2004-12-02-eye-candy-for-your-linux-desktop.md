[SuperKaramba](http://netdragon.sourceforge.net/) is, in simple terms, a tool that allows you to easily create interactive eye-candy on your KDE desktop. Currently, only linux is officially supported.

- RPM for Suse 9.2 are [here](http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php?content=18011)
- A choice of Karamba theme are [here](http://www.kde-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=38)

 [kde-look.org](http://www.kde-look.org/) is worth a visit: You will find under the GPL:  
 Wallpapers, Themes / Styles, Window Decorations, Icons, System Sounds , Color Schemes, Screensavers, Noatun Skins, K-Jofol, Kaiman, Screenshots, Splash Screens, X11 Mouse Themes, KDE Improvements   
 Karamba themes, Fonts, Service Menus, Other, Artwork Contest
