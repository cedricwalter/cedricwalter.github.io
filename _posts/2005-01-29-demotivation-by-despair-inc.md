Some great sentences taken from [despair.inc](http://www.despair.com/demotivators) a company which is selling a lot of calendars, posters, mugs, and gadgets.

-Get to work: You Aren’t Being Paid to Believe In the Power of Your Dreams

-Ambition: The Journey of a Thousand Miles Sometimes Ends Very Very Badly

-Meetings: None of Us Is As Dumb As All of Us

-Idiocy: Never Underestimate the Power of Stupid People in Large Groups

-Mistakes: It Could Be That The Purpose of Your Life Is Only to Serve as a Warning to Others

-Procrastination: Hard Work Pays Off After Time, But Laziness Always Pays Off Now

-Consulting: If you’re not a part of the solution, there’s good money to be made in prolonging the problem.

-Flattery: If You want to get to the top, prepare to kiss a lot of the bottom

-Risks: If You never try anything new, You’ll miss out on many of Life’s great disapointments.

They are also [selling a book](http://www.despair.com/demotivators/artofde.html): "The art of demotivation: a revolutionary new management guide"
