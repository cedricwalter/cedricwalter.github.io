Maybe You do not know or have ever heard that there can be an alternative to notepad, wordpad, textedit, ultraedit,
textpad …and the list of text editor can be very long. I am using [JEDIT](http://www.jedit.org/) since 4 years now and
will never give it back. It is one of these #Java application which demonstrate the portability and quality of #Java
software. (Mac OS X, OS/2, Unix/Linux, VMS and Windows)  
Open source, GNU General Public License, 7 years of development: jEdit is a mature and well-designed multi purpose text
editor. [ Feature list here](http://www.jedit.org/index.php?page=features)
