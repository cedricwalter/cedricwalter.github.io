<span style="font-style: italic;"> Three of the world’s biggest electronics companies — IBM, Sony and Philips — have joined forces with the two largest #Linux software distributors to create a company for sharing #Linux patents, royalty-free.   
<span style="font-style: italic;">The Open Invention Network (OIN), as the new firm unveiled Thursday is known, could mark a breakthrough in resolving how to protect vendors and customers from patent royalty disputes resulting from freely shared #Linux code.
