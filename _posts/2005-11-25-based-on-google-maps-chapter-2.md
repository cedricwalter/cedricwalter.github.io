-Chating rooms: [This example demonstrates bi-directional messaging](http://www.themidnightcoders.net/examples/messageserver/chat/mapchatajax.htm) between heterogeneous (Flash and AJAX) clients and WebORB Message Server. The server performs additional processing to geolocate chat users, injects necessary information in messages so users can plotted on the map.
-Weather Bond:[ Weather Bonk lets you view real time weather information](http://www.weatherbonk.com/weather/index.jsp) on a map. This can provide some very interesting information, particularly in areas with microclimates, such as San Francisco.  
    The Interface do not only look great, but informations displayed as layer are really fantastics since it use also many sources: national weather services. Weather Underground, Weather Bug, Citizen Weather Observer Program, and National Weather Service. [look at the About.](http://www.weatherbonk.com/weather/about.jsp)

It is as default displaying You the weather of Your location (geo ip localization)
