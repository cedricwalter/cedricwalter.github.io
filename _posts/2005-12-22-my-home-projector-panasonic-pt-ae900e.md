You want to read some reviews on the PT900, the best place is at [www.projectorcentral.com](http://www.projectorcentral.com/) in the[ Panasonic PT900 section](http://www.projectorcentral.com/Panasonic_Home-PT-AE900U.htm). I have watch till now only 2 DVD with it, base of image: 2 meters (I have fixed nothing, I expect something like 3.5 when installed), I’ve use it in low power mode: astonishing images!!!!!

- Description: 16:9 LCD Home cinema   
    
- Technology: 3x LCD   
    
- Resolution: 1280 x 720   
    
- Aspect ratio: 16:9, or 4:3   
    
- Picture brightness: 1100 Ansi Lumen   
    
- Contrast: 5000:1   
    
- Cooler sound: 26 (db im Normalmodus)   
    
- Distance: 1,2 bis 7,4 (m)   
    
- Features: Lens Shift, Trapezoidal correction, Smooth Screen Technologie, Prog. Cinema Scan   
    
- Connections: HDMI, YUV, VGA, Scart, S-Video,   
    
- Video Lamp: 3.000 hours   
    
- Measures: 33,5×10,4×27 (cm)   
    
- Weight: 3.6 kg
