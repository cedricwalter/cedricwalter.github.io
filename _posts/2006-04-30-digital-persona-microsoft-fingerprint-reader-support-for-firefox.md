 *<font color="#e65806" face="Georgia, Times New Roman, Times, serif" size="+5">Finger **<font color="#fea034" face="Georgia, Times New Roman, Times, serif" size="+5">fox.***  
A Firefox extension that allows a workaround for the reader software to recognize Firefox as a valid fingerprint capable application. It has finally popup on the internet…  
directLink to the extension <http://redoneill.free.fr/pub/fingerfox.xpi>  
[Site of the author translated to english](http://64.233.179.104/translate_c?hl=en&langpair=fr%7Cen&u=http://redoneill.free.fr/fingerfox/)  
Version is only 0.4 but it seems functionnal!  
 **Usage**: click the "fingerfox" button in your bottom toolbar and then use your fingerprint wizard to have it fill that in, and it will launch the window automatically if you want.
