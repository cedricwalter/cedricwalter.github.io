 Will I buy one? YES!   
Will I resell my XBOX360? maybe as It is too limited for me compare to the PS3

- Do it yourself drive extensions, drive is not encrypted or locked
- No codecs restrictions very important for a media center.
- Linux, ([Patches are already popping at PowerDeveloper.org](http://www.powerdeveloper.org/playstation.php))
- Less noisy

 Expensive? not so much if You consider its free online service ($50 for a gold live account), a 60GB versus a 6GB (+$99 for 80Gb XBOX360), a bluray drive included (+$200 for the HD-DVDrom on the XBOX360)
