| I’ve installed this week end my home cinema amplifier: the [latest ONKYO TX-SR804](http://www.eu.onkyo.com/products/product_en_4430384.html). It has a very good price (1250CHF) value ratio with its 170 watts per channel (under 6 Ohm 1kHz DIN). Unfortunately I was also forced to add 40 meters! of expensive cable(2 x 6mm) for 438CHF (284 euro).   ![](/assets/images/2006/12/expensive-home-cinema-cable.jpg)   It support nearly all sound standards (except THX ultra2 but in a room of 28m^2 it is not really needed) - THX&#153; Select2 certified - THX&#153; Surround EX, DTS®, DTS® 96/24, DTS®-ES Discrete/Matrix, DTS® Neo:6 decoding - Dolby® Digital, Dolby® Digital EX, Dolby® Pro Logic IIx decoding  People looking for an alternative may look at offering from Denon (3806), Yamaha RX-V1700, Pionneer. |
