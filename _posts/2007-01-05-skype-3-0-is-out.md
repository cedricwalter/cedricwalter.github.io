Skype 3.0 is a major milestone release, comparable in its significance perhaps to Skype video calling that came out about a year ago!

- New look and feel
- Public chats
- Firefox Browser extensions
- Sound settings wizard
- Extras like playing games!
- Extras sharing your desktop!
- Extras: use a whiteboard to make notes on skype calls!
- Skypecasts controls
- Enterprise compatibility
