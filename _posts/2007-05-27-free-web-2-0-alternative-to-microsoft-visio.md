 Microsoft should worry, the new wave of Web 2.0 tools show a lot of promises…

Either Microsoft propose some on line/off line free alternative to their flagship office tools or they may even simply disappear in the long term.

You want to create many types of diagrams such as Flowcharts, UI wire frames, Floor plans, Network diagrams, UML diagrams, or any other simple drawing or diagram. But as a home/occasional user are not ready to pay the outrageous price of $400 (Visio 2007 standard ) to $800 (Visio 2007 Ultimate)
