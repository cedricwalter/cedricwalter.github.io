The LittleBigPlanet experience starts with players learning about their character&#146;s powers to interact physically with the environment. There are obstacles to explore, bits and pieces to collect and puzzles to solve &#150; requiring a combination of brains and collaborative teamwork. As players begin to explore, their creative skills will grow and they will be ready to start creating and modifying their surroundings &#150; the first step to sharing them with the whole community. Characters have the power to move anything in this glued and stitched-together landscape; they have the power to design, shape and build both objects and entire locations for others to play. Players can make their world as open or as secretive to explore as they like. When it&#146;s ready, they can invite anyone within the LittleBigPlanet community to come and explore their patch &#150; or can go and explore everybody else&#146;s.
