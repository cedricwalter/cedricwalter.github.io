[Bulbs Unlimited](http://www.bulbs-unlimited.com/home_en.htm) is a system, which transforms European standard light bulbs into various, spectacular light objects. Therefore light bulbs were linked together, like Lego® bricks, by simple snap buttons (press buttons). A custom-built positioning device, “the Inkubator” maintains to fix the snap buttons on defined positions on bulbs surfaces, by using a unique adhesive. The chance to locate snap buttons on several different positions on bulbs surfaces, guaranteed a unlimited number of potential light objects
