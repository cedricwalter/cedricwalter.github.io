 I’ve decide to replace my existing gallery, powered by [Gallery2](http://www.gallery2.org/), at [gallery.waltercedric.com](http://gallery.waltercedric.com) by a [SmugMug](http://smugmug.com/) account, mainly because:

- My server has only 1Gb of ram
- My current traffic is not compatible with the streaming of 12MPixels images.
- [Flickr](http://flickr.com/) and [SmugMug](http://smugmug.com/) both support IPTC tags and EXIF captions embedded in photos.
- I like the graphical user interface of smugmug a lot, look more professional, even more than [Flickr](http://flickr.com/)
- [API well documented](http://smugmug.jot.com/API) and huge number of tools to upload data

 All in all and thanks to the integration in [picasa ](http://picasa.google.com/)though a [smugmug picasa plugin](http://www.dgrin.com/showthread.php?t=71187), it has been very easy to send 7GB (roughly 2000 pictures)

 All pictures (1500 !) of my trip in Australia are now online at [http://cedricwalter.smugmug.com/ ](http://cedricwalter.smugmug.com/)

 If you are lost due to the huge number of pictures, yo’ll like the keyword page <http://cedricwalter.smugmug.com/keyword/>
