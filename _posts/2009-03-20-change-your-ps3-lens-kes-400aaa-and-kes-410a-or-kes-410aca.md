Side by side, here are the PS3 lenses that are currently available for your PS3. [If you ever decide to change the lens yourself](https://waltercedric.com/new/?p=3558), my best advice is to always open your PS3 first before ordering any new lens as there is no way to determine which model use which lens.

**Attention**: PS3 40gb may use a different lens: LENS KES-410A or KES-410ACA (but some ps3 40gb has been found with the other lens KES-400AAA)
