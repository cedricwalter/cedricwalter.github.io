This module displays Smugmug videos using flash and Smugmug API.

[Download Now](https://waltercedric.com/new/?p=510)

This extension has been submitted to the JED (Joomla! extensions) and can be found also under the [category photo-channels](http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/external-contents/photo-channels).

You can see all my extensions live running at <http://demo2.waltercedric.com>
