![Joomla Logo Horz Color](/assets/images/2009/06/JoomlaLogoHorzColor.png "Joomla Logo Horz Color") Some of my extensions have been finally approved at #Joomla extensions directory

##### [Smugmug Random Picture module](http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/external-contents/photo-channels/8950/details) 

![Joomla! 1.5 Native](http://extensions.joomla.org/images/jed/compat_15_native.png) A small module, highly customizable to display picture randomly from your Smugmug gallery. This module displays Smugmug photos using lightbox, lightbox2 or shadowbox.   
Category: [Photo Channels](http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/external-contents/photo-channels)   
[Download](http://www.waltercedric.com/downloads/doc_details/100-module-smugmug-random-pictures.html)   
License: GPL   
Type: Non-Commercial

![Joomla! 1.5 Native](http://extensions.joomla.org/images/jed/compat_15_native.png) This module is a front end for the myriad of parameter that can set for the Smugmug flash applet. [Download](http://www.waltercedric.com/downloads/doc_details/101-module-smugmug-slideshow.html)   
License: GPL   
Type: Non-Commercial

##### [Smugmug Video Player module](http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/external-contents/video-channels/8952/details)

![Joomla! 1.5 Native](http://extensions.joomla.org/images/jed/compat_15_native.png) Another small module, highly customizable to display video from your Smugmug gallery is ready for prime time! This module displays Smugmug videos using flash and Smugmug API.

Category: [Video Channels](http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/external-contents/video-channels)   
[Download](http://www.waltercedric.com/downloads/doc_details/102-module-smugmug-video.html)   
License: GPL   
Type: Non-Commercial
