But what if you want to build your own, powered by [MAME](http://mamedev.org/) emulator?

[MAME](http://mamedev.org/) stands for **M**ultiple **A**rcade **M**achine **E**mulator. When used in conjunction with images of the original arcade game’s ROM and disk data, MAME attempts to reproduce that game as faithfully as possible on a more modern general-purpose computer. MAME can currently emulate several thousand (more than 7000 titles) different classic arcade video games from the late 1970s through the modern era.

### Screen

Search for an Used/New 15’’ inch or bigger LCD screen. If you choose a bigger screen, you’ll have to adapt the size of your small cabinet.

### Use Mame

There is a main board that allow you to use [MAME](http://mamedev.org/) in an optimized package. You can run nearly 3000 games from the past 30 years.

[PCB 3000 in 1 (2,4Ghz) for 359.00EUR](http://smallcab.net/product_info.php?products_id=130&osCsid=v6a98mmodt4ujdljcjrqmj3ha5)

![mame.3000.games.in1](/assets/images/2009/10/mame.3000.games_.in1_.jpg "mame.3000.games.in1") ![](http://smallcab.net/images/pixel_trans.gif)[   ](http://smallcab.net/)Pcb JAMMA to cabinet / Supergun.   
From Space Invaders to Street Fighter III through metal slug series and the king of fighters find or discover such evidence of legends on one card!. This pcb is ‘THE’ own pcb.   
Support (among others) the Neo Geo system, CPS I, CPS II and CPS III.   
Find the complete game list [HERE](http://www.smallcab.net/telechargement/liste/2950in1_gl.pdf)

If you use the above solution, you’ll have to add a video converter CGA (15KHz) or EGA (24Khz) to VGA (31KHz).
