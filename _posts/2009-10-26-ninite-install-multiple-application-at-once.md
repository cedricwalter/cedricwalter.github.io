With The release of Windows 7, you may face the issue that you have to reinstall a huge number of applications (time consuming and annoying). This is where [Ninite](http://ninite.com/), may help you installing your favorite apps again in no time.

Visit [Ninite](http://ninite.com/) site and pick the software apps you want to install and click “Get Installer” at the bottom of the page.

[![ninite.screenshots](/assets/images/2009/10/ninite.screenshots_thumb.png "ninite.screenshots")](http://ninite.com/)

[Ninite](http://ninite.com/) runs on Windows XP/Vista/7 and works in the background 100% hands-free. Best of all, It is completely free for personal use!
