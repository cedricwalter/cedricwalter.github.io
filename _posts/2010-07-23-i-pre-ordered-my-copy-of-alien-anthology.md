- 710 minutes of horror!
- 10 blu-ray!

For now you can pre-order at [Amazon.fr](http://www.amazon.fr/Coffret-Alien-Anthology-blu-ray-collector/dp/B003TP3VH2?ie=UTF8&s=dvd&qid=1279216351&sr=1-1#productPromotions)
