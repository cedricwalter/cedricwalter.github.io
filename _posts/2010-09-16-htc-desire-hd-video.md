I did have the chance to try a HTC Desire (non HD with Android 2.1) 3 week end in a row, and I did really like what i was seeing compare to my iPhone 3GS.

![htc-desire-hd](/assets/images/2010/09/htcdesirehd.jpg "htc-desire-hd")

- Android speed of browser is better, while in WIFI using some sites as references
- Flash 10.1 is working (depends how the site is defining video transfer rates, and pictures quality)
- [Google latitude](http://www.google.com/intl/en_us/latitude/intro.html) and [Google Skye](http://www.google.com/mobile/skymap/) are nice and exclusive to the Android platform.
- [Google Street Maps with voice (aka navigation or turn by turn with voice output)](http://www.google.com/mobile/maps/) is FREE and replace Tom Tom/ Garmin expensive software (yes it do less for now, but you can beat something free that’s already very good)
