This may be my next Digital camera, would like first to have it in hand to see how it compare to a full frame body size (because the Canon 7D while being a DX format is really close in size to the Canon 5D Mark2 full frame body ). As usual, you wont any [better preview than the one of Dpreview.com](http://www.dpreview.com/previews/nikond7000/), even if it’s only a preview. The full review will be a least 33 pages big 🙂

- [DPreview Preview (8 pages)](http://www.dpreview.com/previews/nikond7000/)
- [Lets go digital Nikon D7000 review](http://www.letsgodigital.org/en/27152/nikon-d7000-review/)
- A[mateur Photographer first impressions and hands on](http://www.amateurphotographer.co.uk/news/Nikon_D7000_review_First_impressions_hands_on_and_shooting_news_301884.html)

- [Nikon D7000 flyer](http://www.nikonusa.com/Assets/Digital-SLR/25468-Nikon-D7000/PDF/D7000_leaflet_US.pdf)
- New [battery pack MB-D11](http://www.europe-nikon.com/en_GB/product/accessories/slr/slr-power/battery-packs/multi-power-battery-pack-mb-d11) for the Nikon D7000.

The NIkon D700 offer a dual SD, SDHC, SDXC Card Slot, support for video in both 1080p and 720/30p with continuous AF on video, 39 AF point, 6fps Burst mode, and a magnesium alloy body for less than 1100$
