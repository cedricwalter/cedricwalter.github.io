LG marked the opening of [IFA](http://www1.messe-berlin.de/vip8_1/website/Internet/Internet/www.ifa-berlin/englisch/index.html) 2010 in style by unveiling a giant 3D media façade in [Kulturbaruerei](http://www.kulturbrauerei-berlin.de/), the cultural heart of [Berlin](http://www.berlin.de/), to highlight the upcoming range of devices under the [Android](http://www.android.com/) [LG Optimus](http://www.lg-optimus.com/) series label. This show was done on 3 screen (23 x 21m)
