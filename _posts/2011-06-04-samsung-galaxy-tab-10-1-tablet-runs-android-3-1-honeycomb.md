Second model of [Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1](http://www.samsung.com/global/microsite/galaxytab/) is taking the model number P7100, and will have 10.1-inch display offering 1280×800 maximum resolution for its touchscreen display, and will be running tablet optimized Android 3.1 Honeycomb OS.

The tablet also designed with Bluetooth 2.1, supports quad-band of 3G/4G HSPA+ 21Mbps and GSM/EDGE networks. The dimension of [Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1](http://www.samsung.com/global/microsite/galaxytab/) tablet is 246.2 x 170.4 x 0.9mm, weighing 599g.

- 2MP front camera,
- 8MP rear-facing camera with auto focus,
- 16GB or 32GB internal storage,
- NVIDIA 1GHz Tegra 2 dual core processor
- WiFi 802.11a/b/g/n,
- 6860mAh battery,
- LED flash
- 1080p Full HD video recording
