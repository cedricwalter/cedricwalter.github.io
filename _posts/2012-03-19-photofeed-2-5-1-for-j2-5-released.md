This is a package for #Joomla! 1.6/1.7 and 2.5 that allow you to inline a set of images from your favorite online gallery: [SmugMug](http://www.smugmug.com/), [Flickr](http://www.flickr.com/), [Picasa](http://picasa.google.com/) or any RSS feed in any article.

This is a standalone version of the extension [PhotoFeed](http://wiki.waltercedric.com/index.php?title=Photofeed_fo_Joomla) that is also now part of CedSmugMug
