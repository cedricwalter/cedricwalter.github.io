I have done one last Saturday…for 22.90€ in France

An **[Orthopantomogram](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orthopantomogram "Orthopantomogram")** (OPG) or Dental Panoramic Radiograph (DPR), also known as a "panorex", is a panoramic scanning dental X-ray of the upper and lower jaw. It shows a two-dimensional view of a half-circle from ear to ear. An OPG relies on tomography i.e. images of specific radiographic planes are taken to make up the larger panoramic image. \[[WikiPedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orthopantomogram "wikipedia")\]

I then ask what a MRI [Magnetic resonance imaging](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnetic_resonance_imaging) cost, **only 69€ TTC in Mulhouse** (France 25km from Swiss Borders)
