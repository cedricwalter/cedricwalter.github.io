Got this email from [Cyprian Sniegota](http://deviapps.com), he did develop a #Maven Archetype for easing development of [Joomla](http://www.joomla.org/ "Joomla is one of the world’ s most popular open source CMS (content management system).") extensions. His archetype currently support the creation of a skeleton for components, modules, plugins and templates.

> I noticed some time ago that you described combination of #Joomla! and #Maven. Few weeks ago i wrote joomla-maven-plugin with skeleton projects (sources: [bitbucket.org/deviapps](http://bitbucket.org/deviapps)) based on [php-maven.org](http://php-maven.org/) work.  
> Here is short description <http://deviapps.com/create-joomla-extension-with-maven> and 5 min video (in Polish so far) <http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aE8w9EZciTg>  
> I hope you will be interested.

Thanks to him for having written this project. I will also try to #Maven-ize what #Joomla has done with Ant in the future (I prefer now crystal clear software lifecycle )
