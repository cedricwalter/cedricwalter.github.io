The millenia is         upon us now, and many have converged on the Denius system to greet         the Diving Being.  Without this God of legend, all is dark and empty.         His name is Felden Crais and he knows only one way of doing things.         He is neutral; neither benevolent nor malvolent, but his power is         omnipotent.  No one mortal being is more powerful than he.  (N.Rox Review from DHGF Magazine)                 On a system completely overrun with fighting games, it's usually hard get excited about another one.  This is not the case with Sunsoft's Galaxy Fight : Universal Warriors.  This 170-meg wonder is, truly, the most amazing Neo*Geo game I have ever seen.  From the hyper high-quality FMV intro to the last boss' scaling, warping and color cycling background, Galaxy Fight is total quality, and highly unorthodox : The stages have no boundaries, meaning you can advance or retreat endlessly, adding up to no corner traps and a much larger playfield for defensive players to work with.  Combos come out almost effortlessly in GF (the novice player will have no difficulty whipping out five-hitters), and this is the only fighting game I know of in which two super moves can be two-in-oned into each other, making for insane, beautiful- looking combos.         Galaxy Fight's most impressive trait is its art and special effects - visual effects featured in this title are so amazing that seeing them for the first time is comparable to your first encounter with Gunstar Heroes : Each level features up to five layers of parallax and amazing deep line-scrolling, while scaling in and out!  Power!  It ain't just the backgrounds that are a beauty, though, as Galaxy Fight's characters are animated extremely well and are very unique, not only in design but special moves.         GF's music is another of its strong points : It ranges anywhere from odd ambient tracks with vague snippets of half-heard conversation, Art of Fighting-style, to raging techno and to subdued yet too-cool Japanese Samurai Shodown-esque tunes.  The sound effects are nice too, but the speech is unfortunately spoken with thick Japanese accents.  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- ==============  GAME DETAILS  ==============  BUTTONS :                A - Weak Attack                B - Medium Attack                C - Strong Attack                D - Taunt           There  are  no  defined  kicks or punches in  this  game  for  the           buttons,  the  characters punch or kick when a button  is  pressed           depending upon the situation they are in. INSTANT DEATH MOVE :           Any character can perform an instant Death Move on their opponent.            To do this, turn your characters head and move while pressing the           A, B or C button. (???)            Haven't a CLUE what this means!!!  If anyone does,  please contact           me to update this FAQ with what it DOES mean! COLOR SELECTION :           2 colors are available.  Player 1 is always the normal color for           a  character.   Player  2  is always  the  alternate  color.   So           whichever color you want to be,  you have to play as player 1  or           player 2 accordingly. CONVERT SCORE/TIMER DISPLAY (IN ONE-PLAYER MODE) :           At the start of a new game, press and hold the "start" button be-           fore the round begins to change the score and time displays on the           screen.  Do the same to change back. SPEECH SELECT (IN TWO-PLAYER MODE) :           To choose the ending speech in a VS match, hold either A, B, or C           buttons at the after winning the second round.  A is for small,           humble speech, B for regular, and C for forceful and cheer.  If no           button is pressed, B will play automatically. SCORING :           The scoring categories are as follows : Splendid (You get this if           you beat your opponent in under 15 seconds.), Numbers (I still            haven't figured out what this is for.), Perfect (If you win with            full life meter.), Vitality (The higher your life meter, the more            points.), Narrow (If you win with nothing left on your life            meter.), and Time (The more time left on the counter, the more            points.). OTHER FEATURES :           There are some other features also of note.  There is air juggling.           You can juggle your opponent in the air for a maximum of two to           three hits depending on the character.  You can also attack your           opponent as you fall if you take a hit in the air.  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- =======  CODES ======= BOSS CODE :         US Saturn -> highlight (but do NOT select) VS. mode, hold down left         button, right button, and Y, then press and hold start, while holding         all the above until the character select screen appears.
