He still has his nice priority  jump kick and punch, his Snake Arm is still fast and can be used for quick                       power up with the Snake Fake.   His Guillotine is now like CrazyGlue- it rarely lets go of the opponent in                   the second hit, so use it and abuse it.     His Sand sweep can be done with only the D button now- the B button  changes it into the new Foot Cruncher.  However there probably will not be     much of a use with his old sand sweep anyway since it now pushes the                        opponent far away in a hit.   The Foot Cruncher hits two times- on its way up, and on its way down. The   second hit is unblockable while crouching, and it can be used in combos.  Although it may seem like the Foot Cruncher has quick recovery time, it in  fact can easily be countered with a feirce attack so use it with caution.                                   --==Rugal==--       Do not overestimate Rugal- he is nowhere as strong as in 95', and                  definitely not a character for beginners.   Rugal is at his strongest in a middle-distance. Since his attacks are long   but slow, once an opponent is close up to him he has a hard time getting                 loose -so, always try to be on the offence.   Basically, Rugal should not rely on his special moves and try to use them  mostly in combos. His Reppuken comes out slow, his Kaiser Wave is big but   comes out slower, his Godpress can easily be countered after a guard and   his Dark barrier does not have the insane zero recovery like in 95'. His   Genocide cutter is not a total anti-air but it can be usedful: just keep  in mind that it should be used with B when insterting in a combo, and used                          with a D as an anti-air.    The Gigatec Pressure is also pure suicide when guarded, so it should be     done only in combos or when the opponent is hit with a counter- for   example, right after a Genocide cutter that has switched damages with an    opponent. His other Dm-  the DeadEnd Screamer, is unguardable but too  obvious a move and will easily get dodged by an opponent. Thus, try to use    it to jump over an opponent when they are about to shoot a projectile.            =================================================================    SPECIAL MOVES
