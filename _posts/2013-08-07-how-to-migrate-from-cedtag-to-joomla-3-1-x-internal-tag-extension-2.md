####  Why you may consider migrating away from [cedTag](http://www.waltercedric.com/cedtag "cedtag THe extension for tagging support in joomla 2.5")?

 Joomla 3.1.x has a better build in internal tags solution that differ from [cedTag](http://www.waltercedric.com/cedtag "cedtag THe extension for tagging support in joomla 2.5"):

- [Joomla](http://www.joomla.org/ "Joomla is one of the world’ s most popular open source CMS (content management system).") com\_tags support multi languages,
- [Joomla](http://www.joomla.org/ "Joomla is one of the world’ s most popular open source CMS (content management system).") com\_tags support tags hierarchy,
- [Joomla](http://www.joomla.org/ "Joomla is one of the world’ s most popular open source CMS (content management system).") is build in #Joomla core which mean a clear roadmap and better support.

 It still miss some nice features of [cedTag](http://www.waltercedric.com/cedtag "cedtag THe extension for tagging support in joomla 2.5"), but the may be added by me or somebody else in the future!

 In order to move away from [cedTag](http://www.waltercedric.com/cedtag "cedtag THe extension for tagging support in joomla 2.5") and import all your [cedTag](http://www.waltercedric.com/cedtag "cedtag THe extension for tagging support in joomla 2.5") tags into #Joomla 3.1.x you can follow these steps, [continue reading the how to in my wiki](http://wiki.waltercedric.com/index.php?title=Taxonomies_and_Tags_support_for_Joomla#How_to_migrate_from_cedtag_to_joomla_3.1.x_Tag_component)
