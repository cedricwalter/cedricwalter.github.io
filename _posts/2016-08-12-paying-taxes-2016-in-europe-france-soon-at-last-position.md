Paying Taxes is a joint report by PwC and the World Bank Group. Now in its tenth edition it continues to be a unique
study which investigates and compares tax regimes across 189 economies, ranking them according to the relative ease of
paying taxes.

France now has a higher tax burden than any other country in the euro zone apart from Italy. Even Sweden, emblem of the
Scandinavian high-tax model, takes less in taxes than
France ![Smile](http://www.waltercedric.com/images/wlemoticon-smile_2.png)
