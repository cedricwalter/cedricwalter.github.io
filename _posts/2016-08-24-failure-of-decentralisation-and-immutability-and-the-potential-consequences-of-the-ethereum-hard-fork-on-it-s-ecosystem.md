### Introduction

This talk will be about why complete Decentralization and Immutability matter for public block chains, and why it is
impossible to have one without the other. This will be used as the basis to discuss the consequences of the
recent [Ethereum](https://www.ethereum.org/) Hard Fork, and what it could mean for the viability and integrity of the
platform and its dapp developers.

### Bio

[Avtar](http://www.slideshare.net/arcatomia/ethereum-classic-18-august-2016) has a PhD in Theoretical Physics and
