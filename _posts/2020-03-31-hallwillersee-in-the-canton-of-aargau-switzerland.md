 Lake Hallwil is a lake largely in the Canton of Aargau, Switzerland, located at 47°17′N 8°12′E. 
 It is the largest lake in Aargau and lies mostly in the districts of Lenzburg and Kulm on the southern 
 edge of the canton. The southern tip of the lake reaches into the canton of Lucerne.
