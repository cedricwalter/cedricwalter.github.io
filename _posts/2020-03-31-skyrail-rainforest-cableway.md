Skyrail Rainforest Cableway is a 7.5 km scenic cableway running above the Barron Gorge National Park, in the Wet Tropics of Queensland’s World Heritage Area. North of Cairns, it has won more than 25 awards. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skyrail_Rainforest_Cableway)
