MetaPatterns can be contemplated like a form of understanding the underlying mechanisms of patterns and the form in

The principal objective of patterns is capturing good practices that allow us to improve the quality of the design of
systems, determining objects that support useful roles in a specific context, encapsulating complexity, and making it
more flexible.  
We can observe that the structure of these solutions (patterns) repeats, using a series of basic mechanisms (even in
different levels of abstraction) to produce the same effects in the system.
