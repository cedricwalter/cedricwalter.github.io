In the past 2 weeks, I use to have my watercooling pump (Eheim 1048) making a very bad noise for the first time after
1.5 years of 24/7 operations…and I found this page:

- A german
  one: [<span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">Der Eheim Mod 1+2 -Abhilfe bei lauter 1048/1046 Pumpe ?](http://www.stud.uni-hannover.de/~angho/computer/pc_wak_eheim.htm)
  the trick is to wrap teflon tape around the impellor and then replacing the head back (pictures are self explanatory)

I’ve done the mods and ……lovely the silence of operations is back!!! thanks to all persons which have tried this mod
before! 🙂 it is really working!
