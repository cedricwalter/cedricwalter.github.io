It will include some DIY materials but must be somewhat limited as there are literally thousands of materials to bounce light off of. So, of course, they cannot all be covered here. There are so many variables, they cannot all be covered here. In the end, it is important to try to achieve the greatest benefit for the environment or viewing you want to achieve.
