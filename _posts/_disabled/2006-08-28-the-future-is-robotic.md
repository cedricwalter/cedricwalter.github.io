  A nimble, four-legged robot is so surefooted it can recover its balance even all » after being given a hefty kick. The machine, which moves like a cross between a goat and a pantomime horse, is being developed as a robotic pack mule for the US military. Raibert says the latest version of BigDog can handle slopes of 35° &#150; a steeper gradient than one in two. The hydraulics are driven by a two-stroke single-cylinder petrol engine, and it can carry over 40 kg, about 30% of its bodyweight.
