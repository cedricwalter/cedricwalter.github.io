### A typographic news explorer by Brendan Dawes

> [DoodleBuzz](http://www.doodlebuzz.com/) is a new way to read the news through an experimental interface that allows you to create typographic maps of current news stories.
> 
> "*Over a period of two weeks (though in reality about 40 hours ot total time)* [*DoodleBuzz*](http://www.doodlebuzz.com/) *was developed in Flash using Actionscript 3 with a little bit of PHP – which is needed to create a proxy between Flash and* [*DayLife*](http://www.daylife.com/)*.*"
