## But

In order to better track issues, I still recommend you to create an account as soon as you want to ask or do something serious. Google will rapidly index the site and hopefully also all comments and workaround I will publish there.

### Please

Do not hesitate to post any new features on every component wish list.

### Limitations

You can create issues without login but you will have to register if you want to attach a file.
