> The **Blue Screen of Death** (officially known as **Stop error**, also known as **BSoD**, **bluescreen**, or **Blue Screen of Doom**) is a colloquialism used for the error screen displayed by some operating systems, most notably Microsoft Windows, after encountering a critical system error that can cause the system to shut down to prevent irreversible damage to its integrity. It serves to present information for diagnostic purposes that was collected as the operating system issued a bug check. \[[WikiPedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_Screen_of_Death)\]
