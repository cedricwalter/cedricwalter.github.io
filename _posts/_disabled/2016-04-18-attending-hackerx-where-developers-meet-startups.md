[HackerX](http://www.hackerx.org/) is an invite-only passive recruiting event for developers. It’s a one-of-a-kind opportunity to meet with and learn more about some of the world’s most innovative and fastest growing companies in a casual environment, face-to-face. I will be meeting between 15 to 20 companies in 2 Hours…

### Advantages

- Can have healthy discussions (5 minutes /company) with the companies enrolled in during the speed interview session
- Most of 15 attending companies being startup’s ,opportunity to meet the recruiting/company heads
- To get an insight of recent technologies used by those companies
- Socialize with developers in your area

Apr 28, 2016 [HackerX](http://www.hackerx.org/) – Zurich (Full Stack) - See you there!
