| \| ![](/assets/images/2005/07/seattle-busmonster.png) \| Then Busmonster may be useful for You!  Some individuals are now using the [open source](http://www.opensource.org/) nature of Google Maps APIs to create some customized version, [Busmonster](http://www.busmonster.com/) look very professionnal.  *Seattle Bus Monster helps you find bus stops near you, buses that are arriving at your bus stop soon, and it lets you set alarms to notify you when you need to leave to catch your bus.  Google Maps provides an easy-to-use map interface with detailed road maps and satellite imagery. Trip Planner lets you find bus stops in and around the Seattle area. And, ITS’s Web Services provide up-to-the minute estimates of when the next bus will arrive at many of the bus stops in Seattle. Mix them all together and you get Bus Monster.* Best of all:  The author of Busmonster will release the source code that runs this site soon. 🙂 \| |
