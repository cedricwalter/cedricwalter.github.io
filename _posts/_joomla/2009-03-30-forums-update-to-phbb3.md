 I decide to migrate to [PHBB](http://www.phpbb.com/) and went away from SMF!

 Don’t understand it wrongly, SMF was great for me in the last 3 years: stable, easy to use and not much targeted by hackers 😉

I leave SMF mainly as:

- There is no bridge available in #Joomla! (bridge is discontinued and considered unsafe)
- I would like to unify login across all my sub domains in the near future. The idea is to let you log yourself only once in #Joomla, and have access to [forums](http://forums.waltercedric.com), [bug tracker](http://bugs.waltercedric.com), [wiki](http://wiki.waltercedric.com) and [continuous build](http://continuousbuildserver.waltercedric.com/) and so on,
- [Joomla! forums are also using PHPBB](http://forum.joomla.org/) so scaling should not be an issue (they have currently Total posts 1578894 | Total topics 352041 | Total members 268363) 🙂

# How to go from SMF to PHPBB

The converter plugin found at [SMF 1.1 to phpBB3 Convertor](http://www.phpbb.com/community/viewtopic.php?f=65&t=589439&start=1260&sid=c03ebda0189878b5ec44e7a8d91655d1) did work well for me and has not shown any problem till now.
