![smugmugrandom](/assets/images/2009/06/smugmugrandom.png "smugmugrandom")Another small module, highly customizable to display picture randomly from your Smugmug gallery is ready for prime time!

This module displays Smugmug photos using lightbox, lightbox2 or shadowbox.

[Download Now](http://www.waltercedric.com/downloads/doc_details/100-module-smugmug-random-pictures.html)

This extension has been submitted to the JED (Joomla! extensions) and can be found also under the [category photo-channels](http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/external-contents/photo-channels).

It has the following settings

![smugmugrandom.settings](/assets/images/2009/06/smugmugrandom.settings.png "smugmugrandom.settings")

And you can position using the power of Joomla! template anywhere in your page

![smugmugrandom.example](/assets/images/2009/06/smugmugrandom.example.png "smugmugrandom.example")
