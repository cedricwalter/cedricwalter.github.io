A small module, highly customizable to display a badge of your Smugmug galleries (up to 4 galleries are randomly supported). This extension has been submitted to the JED (Joomla! extensions) and can be found also under the [category photo-channels](http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/external-contents/photo-channels).

Documentation is available [in my WIKI](http://wiki.waltercedric.com/index.php?title=Smugmug_Badge), download it [HERE](http://www.waltercedric.com/downloads/cat_view/333-joomla15/378-smugmug.html) or [HERE](http://www.waltercedric.com/joomla-releases-mainmenu-269.html?QUERY_STRING=#Smugmug%20Badge) and [keep up to date with the RSS feed](http://www.waltercedric.com/index2.php?option=com_versions&catid=18&task=feed) ![](/assets/images/2009/07/feed-icon.png)

4. Native #Joomla! 1.5 module,
5. Code produced is XHTML 1.0 Strict,
6. Tested in the following browser Firefox, Opera, Chrome, IE 8
7. A lot of settings in the administrator backend,
8. Pending Approval at #Joomla Extension JED,
9. GPL v3.0,
10. Non commercial. Administrator panel
