The #Joomla Project announces the immediate availability of #Joomla 1.5.15 \[Wojmamni ama mamni\]. It has been three months since [Joomla 1.5.14 was released](http://www.joomla.org/announcements/release-news/5244-joomla-1514-released.html) on July 30, 2009.

The [Development Working Group’s](http://docs.joomla.org/Development_Working_Group) goal is to continue to provide regular, frequent updates to the #Joomla community.
