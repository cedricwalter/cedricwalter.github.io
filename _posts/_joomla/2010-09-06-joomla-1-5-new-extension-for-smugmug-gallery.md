![smugmug.logo](/assets/images/2010/09/smugmug.logo_.jpg "smugmug.logo") I started writing a new plugin for Smugmug galleries, and after 30 minutes of coding, here are some results!

If you add this in an article at any place, even multiple times a line like

{smugmugrandom uri=<http://cedricwalter.smugmug.com> id=7421071 key=53xgf limit=10 size=200×200}

you’ll get that result, a default CSS will be provided and to let you customized the layout.

[![smugmugrandom.joomla.extension](/assets/images/2010/09/smugmugrandom.joomla.extension_thumb.png "smugmugrandom.joomla.extension")](/assets/images/2010/09/smugmugrandom.joomla.extension.png)

The extension will be available soon, I need time to document [the extension in my WIKI,](http://wiki.waltercedric.com/index.php?title=Main_Page#Smugmug_Gallery) push the extension to my download section, commit the code to <http://code.google.com/p/smugmug-joomla/>
