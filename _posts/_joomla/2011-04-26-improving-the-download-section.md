In order to help you find the right download, I’ve added some new stuff to my blog that should help you find the right download document faster.

- A link to the download section: [Joomla! extensions](http://www.waltercedric.com/index.php?option=com_docman)
- [Search Downloads](http://www.waltercedric.com/component/docman/search_form.html), not the most user friendly but surely the fastest if you know what you are searching for.

- Stay up to date with the download [RSS feed](http://feeds.feedburner.com/DownloadsforJoomla), now with QR code and feed burner support,
- Some new modules 
    - Most downloaded document list
    - Latest downloads
