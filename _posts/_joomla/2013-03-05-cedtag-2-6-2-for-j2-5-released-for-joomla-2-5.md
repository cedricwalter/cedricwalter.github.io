##### ![](/assets/images/2012/05/cedTag.png)

[cedTag](http://www.waltercedric.com/tag/cedtag) provide a useful way to group related posts together and to quickly tell readers what a post is about. Tags also make it easier for people to find your content. Tags are similar to, but more specific than, [Joomla](http://www.joomla.org/) categories.
