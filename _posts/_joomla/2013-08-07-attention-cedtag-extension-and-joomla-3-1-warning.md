###  Remove cedtag before updating to #Joomla 3.1!

 My extension cedtag is now colliding with the internal component name of #Joomla Tag! both are named com\_tags! You wont loose any data by removing cedtag, cedtag databases tables will remains allowing to do a migration later to the internal com\_tags of [Joomla](http://www.joomla.org/ "Joomla is one of the world’ s most popular open source CMS (content management system).")!

 A migration path is documented and will be posted shortly.
