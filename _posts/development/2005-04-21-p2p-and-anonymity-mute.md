![](/assets/images/2005/04/mute.logo_.gif) [Mute](http://sourceforge.net/projects/mute-net) is a file sharing network that uses virtual addresses (instead of IP addresses) and ant-inspired routing algorithms to protect the privacy of both downloaders and uploaders.

- MUTE protects your privacy [by avoiding direct connections with your sharing partners](http://mute-net.sourceforge.net/howPrivacy.shtml) in the network. Most other file sharing programs use direct connections to download or upload, making your identity available to spies from the RIAA and other unscrupulous organizations.
- Ants display collectively intelligent behavior when foraging for food or fighting off predators. Each ant in the colony acts in a rather simple way, but together they end up doing something clever, like discovering the shortest path between their ant hill and a food source. [MUTE’s routing mechanism is inspired by ant behavior](http://mute-net.sourceforge.net/howAnts.shtml).
