<span class="stDeck">[A thin, flexible battery from NEC with a 30-second recharge ](http://news.zdnet.co.uk/hardware/emergingtech/0,39020357,39241049,00.htm)could be an instant hit in a wide range of device markets. NEC has developed an ultra-thin, flexible, rechargeable battery capable of re-charging in just 30 seconds, the company said on Thursday.  
The new battery has been dubbed ORB, for Organic Redical Battery, by NEC, because it uses an organic radical polymer as its cathode. [Read more at news ZDavis](http://news.zdnet.co.uk/hardware/emergingtech/0,39020357,39241049,00.htm)
