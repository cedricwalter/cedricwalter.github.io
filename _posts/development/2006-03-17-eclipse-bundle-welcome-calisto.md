| ![](/assets/images/2006/03/section-java-eclipse.jpg) | The goal of the Callisto Simultaneous Release is to release ten major #Eclipse projects at the same time. We are doing this simultaneous release to support the needs of the ecosystem members who integrate #Eclipse frameworks into their own software and products. While those product producers naturally accept the ultimate responsibility for their customers’ experiences, Callisto’s goal is to eliminate uncertainity about project version numbers, and thus to allow ecosystem members to start their own integration, cross-project, and cross-product testing efforts earlier. Callisto is about improving the productivity of the developers working on top of #Eclipse frameworks by providing a more transparent and predictable development cycle; Callisto is about developers helping developers serve the whole #Eclipse community.  from[ #Eclipse.org Calisto](http://www.eclipse.org/projects/callisto.php#Installing)  I am using Calisto now and YOU? |
