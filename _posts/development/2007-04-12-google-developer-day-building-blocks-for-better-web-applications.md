At Google, we want to help developers build better web applications. That’s why we&#146;ve created APIs and tools to quickly enhance those applications, integrate with Google products and reach millions of users.

That&#146;s also why we&#146;re holding [Google Developer Day.](http://www.google.com/events/developerday/index.html)

Google Developer Day is a chance for programmers around the world to meet Google&#146;s developer product teams and learn something new &#150; be it an introduction to GData or a deep dive into KML. It&#146;s also an opportunity for us to listen to you and hear about what you&#146;re doing today, what you&#146;d like to do in the future and how we can help make that happen.

Whether you&#146;re writing an AJAX application with the Google Web Toolkit, integrating Google services through GData or using Google Maps to create geo applications, we hope you&#146;ll leave Google Developer Day with a new set of building blocks to use in your next project.
