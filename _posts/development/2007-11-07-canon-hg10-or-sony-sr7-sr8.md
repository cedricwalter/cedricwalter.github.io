### Video Playback

 Attention:   
 you must deinterlace video to have the best experience! I use [Elecard Converter Studio](http://www.elecard.com/products/products-pc/consumer/converter-studio/) to view them (free for viewing, use De interlacing:option best for speed),   
 You have to deinterlace because computer monitor are not interlaced!  
 1080i30(60 fields) or 720p60 or 1080p30 have the same raw data rate, while 1080p60 has twice more data!

 I’ve tried 

- [Videolan](http://www.videolan.org/): crash with AVCHD version 0.8.6c)
- [Power dvd ultra](http://www.cyberlink.com/multi/products/main_112_ENU.html) mixed quality output result, some freeze.
- [Sony Vegas 8.0 Platinum edition](http://www.sonycreativesoftware.com/products/product.asp?PID=408) with no success, image not great at all or with artifacts
- [CoreAVC is the best software decoder](http://www.coreavc.com/) out there, install it and any windows application must be able to play AVCHD or H264 (for $8)
