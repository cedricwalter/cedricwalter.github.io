[Browsershots](http://browsershots.org/) makes screenshots of your Web design in different browsers. It is a free open-source online service created by Johann C. Rocholl. When you submit your Web address, it will added to a job queue. A number of distributed computers will open your Web site in their browser. Then they will make screenshots and upload them to the central server!

[![waltercedric.com.browsershots](/assets/images/2009/05/waltercedric.com_.browsershots_thumb.png "waltercedric.com.browsershots")](/assets/images/2009/05/waltercedric.com_.browsershots.png)

Useful if you wan to check your website compatibility (first try to [validate CSS](http://jigsaw.w3.org/css-validator/) and [XHTML](http://validator.w3.org/))
