The base of the cooling fan, when installed, rubs on the drive belt.   
 As a result: the main rotor engaging constantly

Solutions:

- ![](/assets/images/2004/09/ca3505.gif) Buy Kyosho clutch upgrade which has an upper and lower aligner to keep the drive belt positioned.  
     “*Less vibration and better driving efficiency are newly realized. Flange is molded onto the pulley due to prevent slanting of the belt.*” from [Kyosho upgrade page](http://www.kyosho.co.jp/rc_model/caliber/caliber_option/caliber_option-e.html)
