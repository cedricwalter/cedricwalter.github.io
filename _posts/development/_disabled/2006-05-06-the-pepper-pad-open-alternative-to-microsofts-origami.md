 MadPenguin has put an online review of the Pepper Pad, a project similar to the Microsoft Origami solution.

*![](/assets/images/2006/05/Pepper_Pad.jpg)*

*Once again, Tux has beat the Borg to the punch with a new innovation. This time, it’s the [Pepper Pad](http://www.pepper.com/) an ultra-mobile computer designed primarily for video playback and Internet access under circumstances where most notebook computers would be too heavy, and most PDAs would have too small a viewing screen. If you have seen the Origami media blitz (who hasn’t!!) and you want a truly open ultra-mobile computer NOW and don’t want to wait for Origami, you probably will like the #Linux-powered Pepper Pad. [Read more HERE](http://madpenguin.org/cms/index.php/?m=show&id=6827&page=1) at [Madpenguin.org](http://madpenguin.org/) *
