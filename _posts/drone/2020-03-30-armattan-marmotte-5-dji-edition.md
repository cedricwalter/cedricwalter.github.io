The [Armattan](https://armattanquads.com/) Marmotte is the culmination

of everything that [Armattan](https://armattanquads.com/) learned during the design process of their previous flagship
multi-rotors. The design is clean and sturdy, the cage has been redesigned and is entirely in Titanium to protect your
camera.

This new frame is approx 115 grams, made of 4 mm carbon with a life time warranty on all Carbon Fiber and metal pieces!

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-34.jpg)

The tilting mechanism can be oriented to a low 10 degrees or so up to 90 degrees. The front camera position now remove
props from view of the HD stream.

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-32.jpg)

The frame is 5 inch prop compatible, but the longer body means you can more easily fit an HD camera and a bigger lipo,
even at higher tilt. It is definitely bigger compare to my previous [Armattan](https://armattanquads.com/) Chameleon Ti
5” (top).

The longer body means you can more easily fit a GoPro/ DJI HD camera and a bigger lipo (4 to 5S)

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-15.jpg)

## Mounting the Armattan Marmotte

This Bag contains everything needed to build your [Armattan](https://armattanquads.com/) Marmotte 5 Dji Edition

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-1-1.jpg)

A lot of space to mount your DJI Air Unit. A custom 2 sided 3M foams to mount the DJI Air Unit is provided and reduce
damages during impacts.

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-24.jpg)

Zoom on the titanium tilting mechanism that fully protect your DJI camera.

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-25.jpg)

4 screws to secure your flight controller. I personally don’t like the fact that the controller is not in the middle of
the X shape like in the [Armattan](https://armattanquads.com/) Chameleon.

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-26.jpg)

Mounting the electronic together.

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-28.jpg)

The DJI Air System has plenty of space. An extended-aluminum rear provides a protected tail, the raised lips on the
front and rear metal help keep you from damaging the carbon frame.

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-35.jpg)

View on the frame.

![](/assets/images/2020/03/Armattan-Marmotte-5-Dji-Edition-36.jpg)

And now let’s go flying!
