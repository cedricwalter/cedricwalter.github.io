# The Engineer High school of Mulhouse
The [ESSAIM](http://www.essaim.uha.fr/) is proposing to the students of level Bac+2, a formation with the trade of
engineer in the 4 following fields: Data processing &amp; Networks, Systems &amp; Signals, Textile &amp; Clothing,
Mechanics &amp; Systems.
