# How To

| Required:- LED - Resistor 220 Ohms  The resistor (value depending of the LED power) has to be soldered on the longest LED wire  ![](pc/xbox/harddisk_led/im/needed.jpg)                                                                                                                  |     |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----|
| +5 Volts is taken from the connector, you can open the connector and close it without any difficulties   ![](pc/xbox/harddisk_led/im/5voltsPoint.jpg)                                                                                                                                                |     |
| The trickiest part is to solder on pin39 the signal (negative)   ![](pc/xbox/harddisk_led/im/tricky1.jpg)   You should also verify carefully your soldering, this tool may help   ![](pc/xbox/harddisk_led/im/lens.jpg) |     |
| Drill a 3mm hole or bigger depending on LED size   ![](pc/xbox/harddisk_led/im/insideCase.jpg)  Final Result                                                                                                                                                       |     |
