![The NEOGEO X Gold entertainment system comes complete with the NEOGEO X Station](/assets/images/2012/10/NeoGeoXGold-008.jpg "The NEOGEO X Gold entertainment system comes complete with the NEOGEO X Station")

The [NEOGEO X Gold](http://neogeox.com) entertainment system comes complete with the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Station, the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Handheld with 20 pre-loaded NEOGEO classic titles, and the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Joystick. In addition to coming pre-loaded with 20 NEOGEO classic titles, the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Handheld device features a crisp 4.3-inch LCD display, an expandable game card slot, internal stereo speakers, and a 3.5mm headphone jack for a personal gaming experience you can take anywhere. The [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Handheld device works with the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Joystick and [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Station to charge the handheld device and transfer the action directly to a television set or monitor, via HDMI or A/V out, for a true arcade experience right at home! The [NEOGEO X Gold](http://neogeox.com) Limited Edition is only available for pre-orders made before 12/6. The [NEOGEO X Gold](http://neogeox.com) Limited Edition comes with the Ninja Master’s [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Game Card.

As a NeoGeo fans since 1991, I will pre order at least 2 of them if not more ![Winking smile](/assets/images/2011/01/wlEmoticon-winkingsmile.png)

- Limited Edition – Includes the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Ninja Master’s game card.
- Play anywhere – At home or on the go.
- 20 pre-loaded classic Neogeo games.
- Home arcade experience with the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Station when the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Handheld is docked inside and the [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Arcade Stick is plugged in. Outputs to TV via HDMI or A/V out.
- Contents include: [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Station, [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Handheld, One [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) Arcade Stick, and Ninja Master’s [NEOGEO X](http://neogeox.com) game card
- 4.3 inch LCD screen,
- Expandable game card slot, stereo speakers and a headphone port.
