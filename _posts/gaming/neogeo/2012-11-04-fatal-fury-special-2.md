To USE Ryo, reach him (save the game at this point to make using him later easier) and beat him (beating him required?)  Then wait for the demo-mode fights to begin.  After it says "FIGHT", enter the following on the left controller:      Up  Left  Down  Right  Up  A  Up  Right  Down  Left  Up  C      You should hear Ryo's taunt ("Ora! Ora!").  Now press Start and select 1P vs 2P (you may not use him in a 1P vs COM game).  Ryo's picture should now be available, in the lower right next to Krauser. His special moves are listed on the move listing.      Also, if you take a look in the background, Micky, Todo, Lee, and John, (all from Art of Fighting) make little cameos.  Todo is in the dead center of the background, Micky, Lee and John are up in the trees.     The above procedure is for the home game, is selection of Ryo possible in the arcade version?
