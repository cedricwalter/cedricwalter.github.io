"My name is King Lion, the strongest fighter in the world. You  foolish people who think you are strong enough, gather together !   To let you know my power, I will hold a [God of the Beast Fighting  Competition] !! Those who survive in this game can have the right to fight  against me !   If someone can beat me up, he can get billions of money and the honour as  the Hero, his name lives forever...   Hope you won't let me down !! Hahaha ... Ho"   King Lion finish his announcement by punching into the camera and the  2 ton rubbish flies 4 meters away !   Although no one seems to understand the aims of King Lion, but he really  did clearly showed his power to everyone. After this announcement, about  100 fighters in this city gather together.   In King Lion's game, there's no rules, no limitions on weapons, no  specific places. Those who don't have enough power lose quickly.
