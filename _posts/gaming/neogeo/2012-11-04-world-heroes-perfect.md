|=-------------------------------------------------------  World Heroes Perfect is a Neo Geo Beat 'em up game, available in the arcades and on the home Neo Geo systems.  I myself own the Neo Geo CD version, therefore all moves shown have been discovered/confirmed by me.     ------=| GAME DETAILS |=-------------------------------------------------------   FEATURES :  The gameplay is much like World Heroes 2 Jet, with added six-button play (3 punch strengths, 3 kick strengths, & also 4 taunts per character).  All characters also have an ABC Special move, and standard Weopon Killer and Guard Crush attacks.  The game now features a 'Hero' charge indicator, to allow enhanced versions of certain special moves, and also added are last-breath come-back Ultimate Attack moves when energy is low (similar to Fatal Fury Desperations).  In the Home version, there are 3 modes of play;  MVS, CD and VS.  All characters are playable in the VS mode, MVS is Arcade mode.  I do miss the old Death Match option of World Heroes I & II, that's what I think made the original game work and be more individual from all the other beat 'em ups out there.  I also enjoyed spiking my opponent on a wall!  In MVS mode, players fight through each period in turn until meeting the final boss.  The game also now features the ability to do multiple attack hits in the air, and some special moves produce hits of 30+ hits!!!  The general game fighting engine has been improved, allowing for greater combo potential.  Certain moves can be 'buffered', allowing an opponent to be hit as they land/after being thrown. Characters even look up when their opponent jumps!  Usual best-of-three round winning rules apply.    PERFECT POSE:  If a round is won without losing any of your energy bar (i.e: a Perfect Victory), you will be rewarded with a unique 'Perfect Pose' for your character.  Some characters have the same 'PP' as one of their Taunt moves, other characters have some very good poses which would not normally be seen.  Hence the name of the game: World Heroes PERFECT!  Also, I noticed that when I was playing on 'The Period' stage, and I got a Perfect Victory using Kim Dragon, he did his Perfect Pose and a statue of the dog (in the background of that stage) fell from the sky!    COLOUR SELECTION :  Two colours can be selected, using either A or D buttons when selecting a character.  The A button is the normal colour, the D button is the alternative colour.    CHARACTER SIZE :  The game features the ability to play against a minaturised CPU opponent in VS mode.  To do this when selecting a CPU opponent, select them with the B button. The CPU opponent then becames TINY, and all their moves too!  Rasputin's ABC Special Attack produces this effect, as well as Gokuu's Genie- Sucker move.    HERO GAUGE :  Each character has at the bottom of the screen a bar which fills up when your character attacks the opponent and makes contact (special or normal moves, blocked or not).  When the bar becomes full, it flashes and displays 'HERO'.  At this point, your character now has the following abilities;      - MAX Move; a boosted special or Ultimate Attack move       (one or more of the characters special moves becomes stronger,        and your character will flash before it executes).         See each characters section for details.  * is specified by any        special move which can do this.      - Increased number of hits on opponent      - Boosted power of normal attack moves      - Decreased effectiveness of opponents moves on your own character       (you also cannot be knocked out)  The bar will only drain when you have used the move (successfully hitting the opponent or not).  Partly charged bars are carried over to the next round.    ABC SPECIAL ATTACK :  Each character has their own individual form of move/attack when pressing the buttons ABC together (also may be done with a joystick direction nd ABC.  See each character's section for details of these moves.    ULTIMATE ATTACK MOVES :  These are 'come-back' / desperation moves, which can be used any time your character's energy bar is flashing (from too much hit damage).  The effectiveness and appearance of the moves is also altered if the HERO Gauge is full at the time the move is used.  Full details are given in each characters section.     ------=| STAGE DETAILS |=------------------------------------------------------    1 - GENESIS       The creation of the world is witnessed here.  Flows of lava and erupting      volcanoes are everywhere, giving a red/yellow glow to the stage.      Opponents fight on the edge of a great chasm.    2 - JURASSIC PERIOD       Jungle/swamp area, lush green vegetation and dragon flies fill the screen.      Dinosaurs and animals witness the proceedings.  Fight takes place in a      large puddle of the swamp.    3 - THE ICE AGE       Large dinosaur bones and ice create the scene, with breakable bones in      the forground.  A family of rodents run around the screen.  It is      night-time and snow is drifting down slowly.    4 - THE STONE AGE       Fight takes place in a cave, with cavemen eating and watching, Mammoths      circle in the background.  There is a large stone wheel to the left.    5 - THE MEGALITHIC AGE       A very Eqyptian mood to this stage, pyramids in the background and slaves      are hauling huge slabs of stone along.  The fight takes place on a great      block of stone (which is being moved), at sunset.    6 - THE DARK AGE       A dark, foggy dimmly-lit back street at night, with ghostly spirits      flying around the scene, and dark red eyes peering out from dark doorways.      A very spooky stage.    7 - GENROK PERIOD       Fighting on a hill-top dwelling, many different types of people watch the      proceedings.  There are some trees where birds are restings, and a busy      village can be seen in the background.  There is a mountain in the far      distance.    8 - THE INDUSTRIAL REVOLUTION       The scene is a busy train station, a huge steam train dominates.  There      are many people around with their luggage, looking very 18th century.    9 - GREAT WAR PERIOD       A U-boat docking bay at midnight, the moon reflecting on the calm sea.      There is a lot of loading going on; boxes, barrels, chicken crates and      bombs.  Fighting takes place on the deck of a U-boat.   10 - THE PERIOD       In the city street, a huge crowd cheers the fight and throws bottles at      the fighters.  A tall skyscrapper is in the background.   11 - BLACKNESS CASTLE       Zeus's sky palace.  There are two huge sabre-toothed tigers present, and      many armoured knights holding draped lances aloft.  The fight is in a      long hall, a painting hangs on the far left wall.   12 - FINAL STAGE       Blackness Castle has been torn apart, crumbling and deserted.  Fierce winds      blow and lightning flashes.   Before the final stages, your character and Dr. Brown are seen travelling in the Time Machine, but Dr. Brown begins to have trouble, the Time Machine falters and he loses control.  He lands at the sky palace Blackness Castle to fight Zeus.     ------=| BASIC CONTROLS |=-----------------------------------------------------       /====================================================================\     |                                                                    |     |                            (jump)                                  |     |                                                                    |     |                         UB   U   UF        A -
