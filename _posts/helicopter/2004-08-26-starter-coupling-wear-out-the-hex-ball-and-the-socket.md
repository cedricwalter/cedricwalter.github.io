 | **Symptoms**: - Starter coupling does not last long. (CA3018) - Hex adapter from the starter is also rounded |
|---|
| **Reasons**- Starter coupling is made of a soft metal. - Hex starter is sliping at each start and round the starter coupling |
| **Cure**: - Change the part CA3018 (new one will not last longer) - Buy a real hex adapter with a one way bearing like this one:        ![](/assets/images/2004/08/metalHex2.JPG)        Which will last forever, and throw away this kind:        ![](/assets/images/2004/08/hexstarter.JPG) - **Eventualy** you can buy the aftermarket part        [![](/assets/images/2004/08/TN_016-141.JPG)](images/Helico/caliber/correct/016-140.JPG)        Some people has reported good result with it, but it is expensive. Buy this prt only if you want a metal clutch. |
