Bought this Thunder Tiger Raptor 46 on eBay, January 2002

## **Equipped with**

- Blades quick uk CFK blades
- cyclic NES 4000
- all others NES 4231
- gyro CSM 540 + JR 2700 G
- Motor OS46 SXH
- Radio Robbe/Futaba R149DP + Futaba FF8 + 2 \* 1700mAh

A lot of upgrade……
