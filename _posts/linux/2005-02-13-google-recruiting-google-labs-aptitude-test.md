The new edition of #Linux Journal has a special insert: The [GLAT](http://cruftbox.com/blog/archives/001031.html) (Google Labs Aptitude Test) is a Google recruiting quiz presented as a spoof of standardised aptitude tests. It is filled with math and Google-related trivia. On [slashdot,](http://slashdot.org) a lot of users [have tried to find the answers](http://slashdot.org/articles/04/09/19/1949255.shtml?tid=217) to the test.
