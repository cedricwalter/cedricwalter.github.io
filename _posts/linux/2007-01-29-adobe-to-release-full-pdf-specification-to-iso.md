PDF has reached a point in its maturity cycle where maintaining it in an open standards manner is the next logical step in evolution. Not only does this reinforce Adobe’s commitment to open standards, but it demonstrates that open standards and open source strategies are really becoming a mainstream concept in the software industry.   read more at [desktop linux here.](http://www.desktoplinux.com/news/NS2782821882.html) |
