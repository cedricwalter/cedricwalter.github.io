# MeeGo** is a #Linux-based open source mobile operating system project<sup> </sup> which was announced at Mobile World Congress in February 2010 by Intel and Nokia in a joint press conference. Its aim is to merge the efforts of Intel on Moblin and of Nokia on Maemo into one project. It is hosted by the #Linux Foundation.<sup> </sup> Free and Open Source of course.
