| ![](/assets/images/2006/04/opensource.jpg) | Nothing seems to stop the open source shift   LinuxDevices is reporting that non-profit public policy research group, Committee for Economic Development, has released a [72-page report](http://www.linuxdevices.com/news/NS2542131185.html) that takes a look at open standards, open source software, and ‘open innovation.’ From the article: ‘The report concludes that openness should be promoted as a matter of public policy, in order to foster innovation and economic growth in the U.S. and world economies.’ The [full text](http://www.ced.org/docs/report/report_ecom_openstandards.pdf) \[PDF\] of the report is also available for download from the CED site." |
