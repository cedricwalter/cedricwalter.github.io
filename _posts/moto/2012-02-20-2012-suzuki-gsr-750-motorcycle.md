[The Suzuki GSR750](http://www.suzukimotorcycles.com.au/bikes/road/sport-sport-touring/2012/gsr750.html) is ideal for cruising the streets or weekend blasts, as it combines a powerful engine and quality components, with strong angular lines across the whole machine.

Developed from the power plant of the legendary Suzuki GSX-R750, the [GSR750](http://www.suzukimotorcycles.com.au/bikes/road/sport-sport-touring/2012/gsr750.html) features a 749cc, four-cylinder, fuel-injected DOHC engine resulting in broad power band with loads of low-end torque and dynamic mid-range power.
