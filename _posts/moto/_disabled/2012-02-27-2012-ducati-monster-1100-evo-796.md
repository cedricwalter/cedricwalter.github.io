The Monster 1100<sup>EVO</sup> perfectly mixes high engine power, a Ducati Safety Pack (ABS + DTC) contained of advanced electronics, a new sports concept and superior riding pleasure to refine the original Monster formula, which makes it an icon of the naked segment.

The two-valve engine on the Monster 1100<sup>EVO</sup> features a crankcase which significantly reduces overall engine weight.
