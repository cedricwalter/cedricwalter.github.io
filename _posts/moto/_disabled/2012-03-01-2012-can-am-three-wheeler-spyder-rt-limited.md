Can-Am Spyder RT Limited is impossible to categorize beyond the Luxury Touring category. Thanks to a pair of front
wheels, this unique three-wheeler is a fascinating and beguiling curiosity for motorcyclists. The Rotax-powered 998cc
machine puts out 100 horsepower and runs through an optional semi-automatic five-speed transmission.

High technology includes a stability system, traction control, ABS and Dynamic Power Steering. Storage on the machine is
plentiful, thanks to a front trunk with a 35-pound capacity.
