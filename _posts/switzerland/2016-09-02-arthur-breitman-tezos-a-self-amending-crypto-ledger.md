[Tezos](https://tezos.com/) was created first and foremost to address a governance issue in decentralized cryptographic
ledgers. It does so by allowing stakeholders to approve updates to the protocol, including the consensus algorithm, and
the governance rule themselves. [Tezos](https://tezos.com/) is written in [OCaml](https://ocaml.org/) (a functional
programming language) and rely on [Coq](https://coq.inria.fr/) for advanced proof checking and formal verification.
Source code will be opened and available later this year.

Arthur Breitman of "**[Tezos](https://tezos.com/)**" gave this presentation on this new technology on Wednesday, August
31st at Monetas HQ (Zug – Switzerland)
