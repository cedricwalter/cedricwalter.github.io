Dorian will share his experience about building a company with professional tools. These 24 tools will help every
founder to speed up the development of its startup, rather than wasting time to look for the required tools to run a
business.

Dr. Dorian Selz is co-founder and CEO of Squirro. Before that he founded the Swiss search platform local.ch and made it
the market leader in four years.

Previously he was a partner and COO at Namics, the largest e-business consultancy in Switzerland &amp; Germany. He holds
a PhD from the University of St. Gallen and a Master in Economics from the University of Geneva.
