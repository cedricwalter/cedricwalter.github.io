Two lookout points offer scenic views of this well-known, 30-metre waterfall cascading into a gully. The Erskine River
is a river in southwestern Victoria, Australia. It arises in the Otway Ranges and enters Bass Strait to the east of Cape
Otway through the town of Lorne. The Erskine River above the falls is known for its high diversity of native fish
species and low occurrence of introduced species
