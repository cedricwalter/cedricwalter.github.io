Sydney Tower is Sydney’s tallest structure with 309 m and the second tallest observation tower in the Southern
Hemisphere. Construction of Sydney Tower Centrepoint shopping center began in the late 1970’s with the first 52 shops
opening in 1972. The office component was completed in 1974 and the final stage of the complex, the Sydney Tower, was
opened to the public in August 1981.
