The Altes Museum is a museum building on Museum Island in Berlin, Germany. Since restoration work in 2010–11, it houses
the Antikensammlung of the Berlin State Museums.

It is located between Neues Museum and Berlin Cathedral Address: Bodestraße 1-3, 10178 Berlin, Germany
