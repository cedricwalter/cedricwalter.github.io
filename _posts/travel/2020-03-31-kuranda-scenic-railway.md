The Kuranda Scenic Railway is the name for the railway line that runs from the coastal city of Cairns, Queensland, Australia, over the Great Dividing Range to the town of Kuranda on the Atherton Tableland. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuranda_Scenic_Railway)
