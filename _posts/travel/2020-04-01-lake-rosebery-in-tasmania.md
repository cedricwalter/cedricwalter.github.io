Lake Rosebery is a man-made water reservoir located in the West Coast region of Tasmania, Australia. The lake is situated within the northern part of Tasmania’s West Coast Range and is fed by the dammed Mackintosh and Murchison rivers. [WikiPedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Rosebery_(Tasmania))
