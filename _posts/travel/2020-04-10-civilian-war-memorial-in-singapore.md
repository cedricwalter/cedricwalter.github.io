The Memorial to the Civilian Victims of the Japanese Occupation, usually called the Civilian War Memorial is a war memorial and heritage landmark in Singapore next to Esplanade MRT station. It was built in memory of the civilians killed during the Japanese occupation of [Singapore](https://waltercedric.com/tag/singapore/) during World War II. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Civilian_War_Memorial)
