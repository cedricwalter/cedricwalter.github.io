Parkview Square is an office building located in the Downtown Core Planning Area, Central Region, [Singapore](https://waltercedric.com/tag/singapore/). It is situated along North Bridge Road, and is near the major commercial hub at Marina Centre. it is designed in the classic Art Deco style, inspired by New York City’s 1929 Chanin Building. The exterior surface of the building is clad in brown granite, bronze, lacquer, and glass. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parkview_Square)
